Abstract

We know that different sounds and sound patterns are present in the environment. Such different kinds of sounds and their patterns can be used to give solutions to many problems that are being existed in the world. Basically we can recognize sounds in ocean and they can be extracted using different sound detection and classification techniques. By using these techniques, the lifestyle of marine life is being observed and protected by some NGOs that belongs to National Board Of Wildlife. One of such type of solutions which helps the marine animals to have a good and healthy life cycle is our project. The old sound detection and classification technique causes many dangers to the marine life which is a draw back.
